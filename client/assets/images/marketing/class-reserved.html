A few tips for class:

When to log in:
5 minutes before class, 10 minutes if this is your first time.

Setup:

  • Set your computer low to the ground ~4ft away.
  • Download Google Chrome before you join class.
  • Make sure you have an internet connection.

What to bring:
We recommend shorts, t-shirt, and athletic shoes. Introductory sessions are bodyweight only, so don’t worry about equipment. Level One classes require your foam roller and resistance bands. If you are traveling and forget your bands, don’t worry, we can modify movements if necessary.
