About this Studio

Owned and instructed by a NYC Rockette, ABSOLUTE BODY is the PILATES studio of precise movements integrated with mental awareness creating the most effective 45 minute workout possible. Wether it's BARRE class or PURE PILATES you seek, this is the place where inches challenge and change you.

Class Types

  • Pure Pilates

    This 45 minute class explores the pilates method focusing on its core concepts with the goal of improving posture, relieving back pain and tightening all your pesky areas. Come try out a class at my studio!
