Live interaction with a real coach

Our classes are led by one or more of our professional coaches, each of whom meets the highest standards of safety, efficacy, and efficiency.

Group training without leaving your living room

Skip the trek to the gym or studio. Take classes with an instructor and up to 15 teammates in real-time. Since our classes are live, you will find yourself with a new support network, which will help you on your continued path to greater health and happiness.

Instruction from real coaches instead of randos on the internet

BODY coaches are trained experts available to guide you before, during, and after class. Whether it’s clarifying a difficult movement, or advice on healthy meal choices, we’re here to help.

Take a complimentary intro session

The BODY Intro Class is completely free and designed to introduce you to the BODY program and have some fun – all while getting fitter. The purpose of this session is to help us serve you better by getting to know your goals and expectations, fitness level, and experience.
