Step 1: Schedule trial classes

Pick a time

Just pick a time and go with it! Typically early morning or late evenings during the week are ideal.

Organize your space

Pick an open space near your wifi source. Adjust your laptop as needed so clients can easily see you.

Step 3: Start growing your business

One class type

Start with one simple-to-understand class type to simplify the decision making process for early customers.

2 to 5 classes/week

Don’t overwhelm yourself with too many classes. Our most successful studios started with only 2 to 5 classes per week.

Set a price, adjust later

Some studios start with a lower price ($30/mo) and raise it after gaining traction ($70+/mo). You can adjust price later.

Schedule consistency

Clients look for schedule consistency and often desire the same class time at least 2x times per week (e.g. MW at 6a).
